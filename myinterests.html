More About Me Soccer & The Beach

Growing up in Southern California I soon found that the beach was my home. I loved the long summer days with the sun on my skin and the wind in my hair. The beach always seemed to be part of me and I want it to always be part of my life. Soccer is my other love. I first found out about the sport on the school yard. Since then I have played in three different continents, multiple clubs teams, and have learned life long lessons through a universal sport. Soccer is another part of me that will never leave.

Also, I love... Cooking & Listening to Music

I have very fond memories of listening to 80's music around the house, in the car, and anywhere possible. I fell in love with music and how it was able to take me to another world, depending what I was listining to at the time. From Rock n' Roll to EDM I like all ranges of music and what they have to offer. I have to admit one of my guilty pleasures in life is watching the Food Network. I love how food can be expressive while being delicous at the same time.

The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints I believe in Christ!

I am proud to say that I am a member of the The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints. I believe in God and in His son Jesus Christ. I am so grateful to know that I have a divine purpose on this earth and that I can live with my family forever.
